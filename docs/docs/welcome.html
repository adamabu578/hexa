Introduction

In the past, integration experiences used to be cumbersome for developers and support staff, as there were unusual backups and a lack of clarity during the integration process.

As the fintech world is evolving, organisations are beginning to automate and create seamless experiences for their developer communities. hexCore, as an MFB, Fintech and Neobank enabler, has structured its API such that it is easy for developers and non-developers to understand how our APIs work and how to build your digital banking products by leveraging on our Corebanking System.

Our Proposal

hexCore APIs enables Banks (Commercial & Microfinance), Fintechs, Neobanks and other players in the payment space extend digital payment applications to its customers. Our APIs are built to suit varieties of use cases. With the hexCore API partners are able to build products such as: Loan Products, Digital Banking Products, E-commerce, BNPL products amongst others.

Benefits of using hexCore API

🎯 User Experience
Seamless user experience for our clients' developers — clean, predictable APIs with consistent patterns throughout.
⚡ Less Down-time
Less burden on our technical support as they would be able to focus on critical issues rather than integration queries.
🔄 Faster Development Cycle
Faster development cycle and improved time to market for our customers. Our SDKs, code samples, and sandbox environment help you ship in days, not months.

Building with hexCore Core API

The hexCore APIs support varieties of functionalities depending on your use-case. Outlined below are key APIs that are widely used to suit a typical digital banking application:

Account Creation

Create your customers with just one API call. Our account creation APIs enables you to onboard and generate a unique account number for your customers. Our API also provides the flexibility of waiving any of the KYC details based on your KYC policy.

Loans

Build an end-to-end loan process using our Loan API. Your users can make a loan request, get approval as set by your institution, and get the loan disbursed to their designated account.

Fixed Deposit

Build an end-to-end fixed deposit process using our Fixed Deposit API. Your users can make a fixed deposit request, get approval as set by your institution, and get the deposit confirmed to their designated account.

Fund Transfer

Our fund transfer APIs enables interbank, intrabank transfer. Your users can send money to another user within the MFB or account holders in other banks.

Cards

hexCore Card APIs enables your customer request for cards, block cards and also avail the flexibility of determining delivery options of such cards. With just one integration you can issue both batch and instant cards to your customers.

Overdraft

Our Overdraft API your business is able to offer customers the ability to spend beyond their available balance (i.e Users can transfer funds even when their account balance is zero). This API enables flexibility to set limit per customer, set interest and get list of available overdrafts.
