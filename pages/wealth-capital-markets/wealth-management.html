Overview

Leading Intelligent Solutions in Wealth

End-to-end AI-powered wealth management enabling advisors and institutions to grow AUM and deepen client relationships at scale.

The wealth management landscape is being disrupted by technology-savvy investors expecting personalised, digital-first experiences — alongside rising regulatory requirements and fee compression. Wealth managers and private banks need a platform built for this new reality.

Hexatech Wealth Management is a fully composable, AI-powered wealth platform built on eHEX architecture. It gives relationship managers a 360° client view, real-time portfolio analytics, and AI-generated next-best-action recommendations — while delivering clients a hyper-personalised digital experience.

From client onboarding and goal planning through portfolio construction, rebalancing, and regulatory reporting — every workflow is automated, compliant, and intelligent.
