Overview

A Unified Platform for Brokerage and Securities Operations

eHEX Brokerage and Securities delivers a cloud-native, end-to-end brokerage operations platform with compliance built in across all markets.

Capital markets firms face relentless pressure — tighter margins, rising regulatory scrutiny, and the need to process millions of orders per second with zero tolerance for failure. Legacy brokerage platforms are not built for this reality.

Hexatech Brokerage is an ultra-low latency, AI-native brokerage and securities platform for the most demanding institutional and retail trading environments. From order management to settlement, from margin management to regulatory reporting — every function is designed for speed, accuracy, and full compliance.

Whether you are building a retail trading platform, a prime brokerage operation, or an institutional OMS — eHEX Brokerage provides the composable building blocks to get to market fast and scale without limits.

Purpose-Built Capabilities for Brokerage & Securities

Five core capability modules purpose-built for institutional and retail brokerage operations at any scale.

High-performance OMS supporting all order types, execution algorithms, and direct market connectivity across 13 asset classes.
  • Multi-asset order management
  • Smart order routing (SOR)
  • Full execution algorithm library
Professional dealer terminals for equities, fixed income, FX, and derivatives — with Level 2 market data, one-click order entry, and live P&L.
  • Level 2 market data display
  • One-click order entry
  • Real-time position and P&L dashboard
Real-time pre-trade and post-trade risk management engine with AI-powered anomaly detection and automated margin call workflows.
  • Comprehensive pre-trade risk checks
  • Real-time position limit monitoring
  • Automated margin call generation
Automated settlement, clearing, and reconciliation with direct CSD and CCP connectivity and automated break identification.
  • T+1 and T+2 settlement management
  • CSD and CCP direct connectivity
  • Automated break identification and resolution
Automated processing of corporate actions, IPO allotments, rights issues, and dividend reinvestment programmes.
  • Corporate action notification and entitlement
  • Automated allotment processing
  • Dividend and coupon payment automation
