Overview

From Incoming Payments to Clear Cash Positions

The fastest path from settlement to insight — virtual accounts that tell you exactly where every dollar sits, in real time.

Virtual Accounts Management (VAM) enables banks and their corporate clients to create sophisticated account structures that mirror their organizational hierarchies — without the cost or complexity of opening real accounts for every entity.

Hexatech Virtual Accounts Management is a fully composable VAM platform built on the eHEX architecture. It enables banks to offer their corporate treasury clients real-time cash visibility, automated reconciliation, and intelligent fund routing — all managed from a single, unified control plane.

From onboarding a multi-subsidiary corporate to reconciling thousands of daily inbound payments, every workflow is automated, auditable, and always-on.

Capabilities

Accept and Optimise Payments at Scale

Seven end-to-end capability pillars that enable banks to offer world-class virtual account services to their corporate clients.

Empower corporate clients to self-create, configure, and manage their virtual account structures directly — without bank operations involvement.
  • Self-service VA creation via web portal and API
  • Corporate-defined hierarchy and naming conventions
  • Real-time account status and balance monitoring
  • Role-based access control for treasury teams
Sub-second balance updates across every virtual account level — from transaction leaf nodes to the master physical account — with full ledger integrity.
  • Real-time credit and debit posting at every VA level
  • Intraday liquidity position reporting
  • Automated interest calculation per VA level
  • Full audit trail on every balance movement
Route and reconcile payments using any combination of account numbers, payment references, IBAN structures, or custom identifiers defined by the corporate.
  • IBAN, sort code, and custom reference routing
  • Multi-bank payment instruction handling
  • Configurable routing rule engine
  • Exception management and manual override
AI-powered reconciliation engine matches inbound payments to open receivables with 99.8% straight-through rates — automatically resolving exceptions using configurable matching logic.
  • Multi-attribute payment matching (reference, amount, date, counterparty)
  • Fuzzy matching for partial and near-match reconciliation
  • Automated exception queuing and resolution workflow
  • Reconciliation analytics and hit-rate reporting
Sophisticated interest calculation at every level of the virtual account hierarchy — supporting notional pooling, zero balancing, and interest netting arrangements.
  • Notional pooling with tier-based interest rates
  • Zero-balance sweeping automation
  • Interest netting across credit and debit positions
  • Automated interest accrual and posting
Configurable fee engine supporting transaction-based, account-based, and value-based charging structures — fully automated and audit-ready.
  • Per-transaction, per-account, and volume-based fee structures
  • Automated monthly billing and invoice generation
  • Revenue sharing arrangements with corporates
  • Fee transparency reporting for clients
Expose virtual account capabilities as embedded banking APIs — enabling corporates and fintechs to build VAM-powered products for their own customers.
  • Embedded finance API for corporate treasury portals
  • EaaS (Everything-as-a-Service) packaging for fintech partners
  • White-label virtual account portal for corporates
  • Webhook events for real-time payment notifications
The Advantage: Transform your Virtual Account infrastructure into a revenue-generating API product for fintechs and corporates building on your platform.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Virtual accounts are unique account identifiers linked to a single physical bank account. They allow corporates to create an unlimited number of virtual sub-accounts that mirror their organizational structure — enabling precise cash allocation, automated reconciliation, and real-time visibility without the cost of opening multiple physical accounts.
The platform supports single-level, multi-level, and unlimited-depth hierarchical virtual account structures. Supported configurations include collections pooling, subsidiary management, project-based accounts, and notional pooling arrangements. Each structure can span multiple legal entities and currencies.
Our AI-powered reconciliation engine uses multi-attribute matching (reference number, amount, date, counterparty) with fuzzy logic for near-matches. Banks typically achieve 98–99.8% straight-through reconciliation rates within the first 90 days. Unmatched items are automatically routed to a configurable exception queue with AI-suggested resolution actions.
Yes. The platform supports multi-bank virtual account consolidation, enabling corporates to see positions across multiple banking relationships in a single view. For BaaS scenarios, the platform exposes all VAM capabilities as APIs, allowing banks to embed virtual account services directly into corporate treasury platforms and fintech products.
The platform maintains a full, immutable audit trail of every virtual account event — creation, modification, balance movement, and interest calculation. All client activity is captured in compliance-ready formats supporting AML monitoring, regulatory reporting, and internal audit requirements across all supported jurisdictions.
Standard VAM implementations go live in 8–14 weeks depending on the complexity of the virtual account hierarchy and number of core banking integrations required. Our pre-built connectors for major core banking platforms (Temenos, Finacle, Flexcube, and our own eHEX core) significantly reduce integration timelines.
