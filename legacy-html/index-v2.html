Why Hexatech

Increase speed-to-market and reduce
cost of banking transformation

Hexatech enhances your development workflow through 700+ composable microservices. These capabilities expand banking functionality, providing a tailored experience that suits every institutional need.

⚡
eHEX Platform
The eHEX core powers your banking operations with high-performance microservices — the core technology for building and running composable banking at scale.
🤖
Contextual AI
The AI layer offers a robust command-line tool for precise control over your banking workflows. Automate tasks, and integrate Hexatech seamlessly into your operations.
🧩
Composable by Design
Hexatech simplifies the process of deploying and managing composable banking modules. Define complex setups with a single configuration file.
🏗️
Build Once, Deploy Everywhere
Hexatech Build is a powerful tool within the eHEX platform that simplifies the process to package and ship your code to any institution on any cloud, on any scale.
🌐
Multi-Entity Orchestration
Built-in multi-entity management with support for multiple countries, currencies, and regulatory environments from a single platform instance.
📊
Real-Time Data Visibility
Hexatech provides a robust solution for managing and sharing real-time banking data. Securely manage positions for intraday visibility and data portability.
🔗
Synchronised API Ecosystem
3,061+ open APIs enable real-time sharing and synchronisation of capabilities across your institution, improving collaboration and consistency.
🛡️
Enterprise Security
Hardened Hexatech deployment includes advanced security features to safeguard your development environment — ISO 42001, ISO 27001, PCI DSS certified.
🔌
Purple Fabric Marketplace
Custom extensions tailored to your specific requirements. Customise and enhance your banking environment with specialised tools and integrations available exclusively through the marketplace.
