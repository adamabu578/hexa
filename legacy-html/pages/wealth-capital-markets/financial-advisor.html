A Unified Workbench for Relationship-Led Advisors

The world's first AI office for relationship managers — combining client intelligence, portfolio analytics, and workflow automation in one workspace.

Relationship managers are overwhelmed with manual tasks, fragmented systems, and compliance obligations that eat into client time. The result: smaller client books, lower satisfaction, and higher advisor attrition.

Hexatech Wealth Financial Advisor is a purpose-built RM workbench that puts AI-powered intelligence at the fingertips of every advisor. Complete 360° client view, AI-generated recommendations, automated compliance documentation, and seamless workflow management — from a single interface.

The result: advisors manage more clients, deliver better outcomes, and spend more time on what actually matters — building lasting client relationships.
