Overview

An Operating Model for Banking in Motion

Purpose-built to give financial institutions the agility, scale, and innovation needed to thrive in open finance ecosystems.

The banking landscape is undergoing its most profound transformation in decades. Customers demand instant, intelligent, personalised financial experiences. Regulators require transparency, auditability, and resilience. Competitors — challenger banks and embedded finance players — move at software speed.

Hexatech Consumer Core Banking is engineered for this reality. Built on the eHEX microservices architecture, it gives you the power to launch new products in days, integrate with any ecosystem partner via 2,000+ open APIs, and serve millions of customers without compromise on performance or compliance.

From onboarding to cross-sell, from savings to lending, from mobile to branch — every customer touchpoint is orchestrated through a unified, real-time data model that puts intelligence at the heart of every interaction.

Capabilities

Beyond Rigid Constraints,
Architecting Customer-Centricity

Our platform principles unlock capabilities that rigid legacy cores simply cannot deliver.

Build and launch financial products in days, not months. The product factory supports current accounts, savings, fixed deposits, recurring deposits, and multi-currency accounts — all configurable without code.
  • Visual product designer with real-time preview
  • Rules-based interest, fees, and charges engine
  • Parameterised eligibility and limit management
  • A/B test product variants before full rollout
Orchestrate end-to-end business processes without hard-coded workflows. Every journey — onboarding, KYC, account servicing, closure — is configurable via a visual process designer.
  • No-code journey builder with drag-and-drop steps
  • Dynamic branching based on customer segment or risk
  • Straight-through processing for high-volume operations
  • Full audit trail on every process execution
2,000+ REST APIs expose every function of the platform to your channel apps, partner fintechs, and internal systems. Build once, connect everywhere.
  • OpenAPI 3.0 specification for every endpoint
  • Webhook-based real-time event notifications
  • GraphQL support for flexible data querying
  • Sandbox environment for accelerated integration testing
Multi-level customer hierarchy supports individual, household, SME, and corporate relationships on a single platform. Configure relationship-based pricing and entitlements.
  • Individual, household, and group account structures
  • Relationship-based fee waivers and reward tiers
  • Consolidated statements across all linked accounts
Every financial event — transaction, balance change, limit breach — is processed in real-time and published to your event bus for downstream consumption.
  • Sub-100ms transaction processing latency
  • Real-time balance and position visibility
  • Event streaming to Kafka, Azure Service Bus, or custom brokers
Embedded analytics, AI models, and operational dashboards give your operations teams the intelligence to act proactively and your leadership real-time performance visibility.
  • Pre-built operational KPI dashboards
  • AI-driven anomaly detection and alert routing
  • Customer churn prediction and next-best-action recommendations
Stay compliant across every market you operate in. The regulatory engine handles jurisdiction-specific product rules, reporting obligations, and statutory calculations automatically.
  • IFRS 9 provisioning and Basel III reporting
  • Country-specific tax calculation (WHT, VAT, stamp duty)
  • Real-time regulatory reporting to central banks
  • Audit-ready transaction logs with full lineage
