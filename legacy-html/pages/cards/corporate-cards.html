Capabilities

Comprehensive Platform Capabilities

Built for the full spectrum of banking operations — configure exactly what you need.

Full suite of corporate card products from commercial credit to virtual cards and expense management.
  • Corporate credit and charge cards
  • Virtual card issuance for procurement
  • Ghost cards for travel and entertainment
Automated corporate card underwriting with financial analysis and risk assessment.
  • Financial statement analysis and spreading
  • Group-level credit limit assessment
  • KYB and sanctions screening
Configurable delegation framework for corporate card programme administration.
  • Hierarchy-based card programme structure
  • Delegate access and approval authority
  • Cardholder entitlement management
Multi-level account hierarchy supporting complex corporate structures.
  • Parent-subsidiary account structures
  • Cost centre allocation and reporting
  • Consolidated billing and payment
Flexible credit facility structures supporting revolving, charge, and budget card programmes.
  • Dynamic credit limit management
  • Temporary limit increase workflow
  • Group-level credit pool management
Automated employee card lifecycle from onboarding to termination.
  • Bulk card issuance and personalisation
  • Automated cancellation on HR offboarding
  • Lost and stolen replacement management
Flexible billing and statement generation for corporate card programmes.
  • Individual and consolidated statements
  • Multi-currency billing
  • Custom billing cycle configuration
Centralised payment processing for corporate card obligations.
  • Direct debit and ACH payment processing
  • Payment allocation across accounts
  • Early payment and settlement workflows
