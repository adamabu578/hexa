Ullas Trust

Nurturing Potential. Enabling Futures.

Nurturing Young Minds, Inspiring Future Leaders

Founded in 1997, Ullas Trust is dedicated to promoting education and nurturing academic excellence among students from economically challenged backgrounds.

The programme supports students from classes 9 to 12 through scholarships, mentoring, enrichment and resource opportunities — helping them build confidence, aspiration and the skills beyond academic barriers.

Mission Samridhdhi

Community-Led Growth. Sustainable Rural Prosperity.

Growing Together in Community for Better

Mission Samridhdhi is a holistic rural development initiative focused on scale — on micro communities from State-Municipal system (rural status of a Community Development programme) that integrates economic, social and institutional development.

Three Guiding Principles:
  • ✓Livelihoods: rural economics
  • ✓Empowering people, processes and institutions

School of Design Thinking

Shaping Human-Centric Innovators of Tomorrow

Preparing for All Progress from Better

The School of Design Thinking is an initiative by Hexatech focused on enabling next-generation entrepreneurs who will be leading the country's next wave of fintech and digital platform innovation.

Through national workshops, academic partnerships, the School aims to catalyse the advancement, innovation and empowerment of next-generation talent in the design and fintech platform community.
